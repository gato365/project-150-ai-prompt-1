How to Submit Your ChatGPT Conversation

STAT 252 · Spring 2026 · Treatment Groups Only (No Structure & Structure)

Part 1 — Copy your conversation
1

Go to your CSU ChatGPT tab

Switch to the tab where you had your conversation at chat.calstate.edu. Make sure you can see all the messages you sent and received.

2

Select all text in the conversation

Click anywhere in the chat window, then press:

Mac:   ⌘ Command + A
Windows: Ctrl + A

This selects everything visible on screen. Scroll down first if your conversation is long, so all messages are loaded.

3

Copy the selected text

Mac:   ⌘ Command + C
Windows: Ctrl + C

Alternatively, right-click the selected area and choose Copy.

Part 2 — Submit via Microsoft Forms
4

Open the submission form

Go to Canvas → Modules → AI Experiment → Submit Your Prompts. Click the Microsoft Forms link to open it.

5

Enter your participant code

The form will ask for your 4-digit participant code — the same one you used to start the activity. This links your submission to your data.

Your code: write it here → ____

6

Paste your conversation

Click the large text box labeled "Your Conversation" and paste:

Mac:   ⌘ Command + V
Windows: Ctrl + V

The full conversation should appear. Include everything — your prompts and the AI's responses.

7

Submit the form

Click Submit. You should see a confirmation screen. You're done with this step.

After submitting
8

Complete the Post-Test on Canvas

Go to Canvas → Quizzes → Post-Test: One-Way ANOVA. Complete it before the end of class. It takes about 8–10 minutes.

⚠️ Control group: If your code starts with 1, you are in the control group and did not use ChatGPT — skip steps 1–7. Go directly to the Post-Test on Canvas.
